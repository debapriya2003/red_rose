Objectives and Commitments

To ensure modern Teaching, Learning & Evaluation process and to minimize the gap amongst students, school and the parents, the school either has implemented or going to implement the following objectives & methods.
  • Latest teaching learning techniques & methods like Interacting sessions, counselling sessions, and group discussions will practice in intellectual regular frequency not only to generate intellectual growth among the students but also to know them completely, their likes, dislikes, their inherent potentials etc.
  • To bring out fundamental change in the method of teaching so that even weak students gets special attention, can feel free to share & to get rid of their problems which ultimately help them to keep away from menace of private tuitions.
  • Every student is regularly assessed and will be given a package of adequate amount of academics along with the provision for developing non-academic skills and talents. The time table is framed in such a way that student can enjoy the right kind of mixture of both academic and non-academic activities. The education imparted here is characterized by thoroughness, high principles and with devotion which helps the students to grow responsibility is of paramount importance.
  • Feedback method in its extensive form has been practiced where opinion, advices and suggestions from every corner including students parents teachers, non-teaching staff, well-wishers & from academicians of repute are sought and thereby considerations and modification are made according to need.

Parent-Teacher Meet/Modes of Communication

  • A regular school parent-teacher interaction is essential for the systematic following up of a child.

  • The school arranges special Parent-Teacher Meetings quarterly where the presence of a parent is of utmost importance. A unique feature at R. R. Management remains present at PTM and evaluates personally all the proceedings of the meeting and steps taken accordingly.

  • Remaining absent in PTM is always considered a shortcoming. Absent in three consecutive PTM without prior permission from the academy will lead to the suspension of his/her ward from the school.
