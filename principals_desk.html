FROM THE TABLE OF MANAGING DIRECTOR

Education is a process of gathering knowledge by developing the power of intelligence conscience and ability of judgement through continuous evaluation and construction of experiences and skills. Education develops and polish the inherent potentials of one self for his personnel as well as social and national development. Education is that light which eradicates all darkness and enlights the society and the social beings. It is the education which lead us to the modern civilization from the primitive darkness. Moreover the primary education is the pillar of formation of develop nation.

Red Rose Academy aims to give education to the students to its entirety by nourishing and nurturing the inherent ability of the student to their excellence in all respect to face every crucial field of life. It is our inherent effort with the help of efficient energetic and sincere, teaching staff to provide advance and innovative education to the generation to succeed in every aspect of life. Knowledge is power and our institution emphasising from the core of its heart in generating this power and the society at large by way of comprehensive education. The education of the institution shall not restricted to the bookish knowledge but also it will impart education to strengthen the moral and ethical foundation and to develop their personality and extra curricular activities.

I would like to extend my heartfelt to my colleagues, patrons of the area, educationist, advisor and staff for their cooperation, support, dedication to materialised and the Management of Red Rose Academy will remain ever grateful to them.

I expect cordial support co-operative and suggestion from them during the process to achieve our cherished goal.

Akmal Hussain Barbhuiya
Managing Director
