Admission Information

Admission Process Admission in LKG to Class IX will be opened from 21st October to 15th January. However seats are extremely limited, so it will be based on interview and a first come first service process. The date of interview shall be intimated at the time of submission of admission forms or candidates seeking admission in these classes can be aware of the interview date from school board. Seats are limited, the decision of principal is final on any matter relating to admission.
Documents Required
  1. Birth Certificate
  2. Three copies of recent passport size photographs.
  3. Transfer certificate and marksheet (for all classes other than Nursery/LKG)
  4. Blood group certificate
  5. Medical certificate
Admission Every existing student has to renew their admission by paying annual fees in the next higher class after passing out from their existing class within 10 days from the declaration of the annual examinations result. They will not be needed to submit any other document except mark sheet of the previous class.
Internet Facility Internet facility available in the campus.
Withdrawals Parent/guardians may withdraw their wards during the academic year subject to genuine cause on payment of fees for the whole academic year. However principal/school authority reserves the right to intimate for the withdrawals of any child if anybody is found guilty of misconduct against the decorum of the school or if any parents/guardians/relatives/friends of any student are guilty of misconduct against the decorum of the school.
